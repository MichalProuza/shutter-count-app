Aimed at landscape, architecture, and studio photographers, it is Sony's definitive resolution-first body — and its shutter rating reflects its professional build quality: 500,000 actuations, more than three times the rated life of the entry-level A7 IV.

How Does ShutterCount Read the A7R V Shutter Count?

Like all Sony ARW bodies, the A7R V stores the shutter count encrypted in MakerNote tag 0x9050. ShutterCount applies the cubic residue decryption algorithm (modulo 249) and reads the 32-bit count from byte offset 0x003a within the decrypted block — the same offset used by the A7 IV, A7 III, A9 III, and A1.

All decryption happens locally in your browser. The ARW file is never transmitted anywhere.

Does the electronic shutter count?

No. The A7R V's electronic shutter mode does not engage the mechanical curtain and is not counted. Only mechanical and EFCS exposures increment the counter. The A7R V's electronic shutter is particularly popular for vibration-free landscape photography — photographers who frequently use it may have a lower mechanical count than their total frame count suggests.

Sony A7R V Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Sony A7R V?

Shoot an ARW RAW file, then drop it into shuttercount.app. The tool decrypts and reads the count from MakerNote tag 0x9050 in your browser.

What is the rated shutter life of the Sony A7R V?

Sony rates the A7R V at 500,000 actuations — more than three times the A7 IV (150,000), and matching the Sony A9 III and A1.

Is 200,000 actuations a lot for a Sony A7R V?

At 200,000 actuations on a 500,000-rated body, the A7R V has used 40% of its rated lifespan — moderate use. There is substantial life remaining. For comparison, 200,000 shots on an A7 IV (rated 150,000) would mean the shutter has exceeded its rated life.

Can the shutter count on a Sony A7R V be reset or faked?

Only a Sony authorised service centre can reset the hardware counter after a physical shutter replacement. Always verify from an original ARW file, not a screenshot.
