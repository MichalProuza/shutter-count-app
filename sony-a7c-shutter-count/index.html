Sony A7C — Shutter Rating & Compact Full-Frame Context

The Sony A7C (ILCE-7C) is Sony's original compact full-frame mirrorless camera, launched in 2020. It takes the 24.2 MP back-illuminated full-frame sensor from the A7 III and places it in a dramatically smaller, rangefinder-style body — the smallest and lightest full-frame mirrorless available at launch. The mechanical shutter is rated at 200,000 actuations. While that matches the A7 III's rating, it's lower than the A7 IV (300,000) and the A7C II that replaced it (300,000).

How to Check Shutter Count on the Sony A7C

  1. Take a still photo with your Sony A7C using the mechanical shutter and locate the .ARW file.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser.
  3. Drag the ARW file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The mechanical shutter count appears instantly, decrypted from the ARW MakerNote in your browser.

Make sure the photo was taken in mechanical shutter mode, not silent / electronic shutter mode. Electronic shutter frames do not increment the mechanical counter.

How Does ShutterCount Read the Sony A7C Shutter Count?

The Sony A7C stores its mechanical shutter count encrypted in ARW MakerNote tag 0x9050. ShutterCount applies Sony's cubic residue decryption algorithm (modulo 249) and reads the count from byte offset 0x003a within the decrypted block. All decryption and parsing is performed locally in your browser — no file data leaves your device.

Sony A7C Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Sony A7C?

Shoot a mechanical ARW frame and drop it into shuttercount.app. The count is decrypted from tag 0x9050 at offset 0x003a in your browser, instantly and privately.

What is the rated shutter life of the Sony A7C?

Sony rates the A7C at 200,000 actuations — the same as the A7 III but lower than its successor, the A7C II (300,000).

Should I buy a used A7C or the newer A7C II?

If budget allows, the A7C II offers meaningful upgrades: 33MP vs 24.2MP, far better AI subject tracking autofocus, 4K/60p video, a 300,000-actuation shutter, and a slightly larger body with an actual viewfinder option. If you find a well-priced used A7C in good condition, it remains an excellent compact full-frame camera for travel and everyday photography.

Does the Sony A7C's silent shutter mode affect the count?

Silent / electronic shutter mode does not use the mechanical shutter and does not increment the counter. Only mechanical shutter frames count toward the 200,000 rated life.

Can the Sony A7C shutter count be reset?

Only Sony authorised service centres can reset the hardware counter. Always verify from an original ARW file, not a screenshot or JPEG.
