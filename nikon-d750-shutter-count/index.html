Nikon D750 — Shutter Rating & DSLR Context

The Nikon D750 (2014) was one of Nikon's most beloved full-frame DSLRs — lighter and more affordable than the D810 while offering a tilting LCD, 6.5fps burst, and excellent video at the time. Its 24.3 MP full-frame CMOS sensor with outstanding high-ISO performance made it a favourite for wedding, portrait, and event photographers. The mechanical shutter is rated at 150,000 actuations. Now over a decade old, it occupies a sweet spot on the used market: capable full-frame DSLR at very low prices.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Nikon D750

  1. Take a still photo with your Nikon D750 and locate the .NEF file.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser.
  3. Drag the NEF file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The mechanical shutter count appears instantly, read from MakerNote tag 0x00A7 in your browser.

Unlike modern cameras with electronic shutter modes, the D750 uses a conventional mechanical focal-plane shutter for every shot — making the shutter count a direct and complete record of total usage.

How Does ShutterCount Read the Nikon D750 Shutter Count?

The Nikon D750 stores the shutter count in NEF MakerNote EXIF tag 0x00A7 (decimal 167) — the same standard Nikon tag used by every Nikon Z-series camera and the D850. ShutterCount reads this tag directly from the NEF file in your browser. No encryption processing is required.

Nikon D750 Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Nikon D750?

Shoot a NEF frame and drop it into shuttercount.app. The count is read from tag 0x00A7 in your browser, instantly and privately.

What is the rated shutter life of the Nikon D750?

Nikon rates the D750 at 150,000 actuations. The D850 has a higher 200,000-actuation rating. Many D750 shutters go beyond 150,000 with careful use, but risk increases after the rated limit.

Is the Nikon D750 worth buying used in 2025?

For photographers with existing F-mount lenses, or those new to full-frame who want to minimise cost, the D750 offers excellent value. The 24.3 MP sensor with its well-regarded high-ISO performance and dynamic range holds up well today. Prioritise units with low shutter counts and clean bodies — avoid those above 100,000 actuations unless priced to reflect the risk.

Can the Nikon D750 shutter count be reset?

Only Nikon authorised service centres can reset the hardware counter. Always verify from an original NEF file.
