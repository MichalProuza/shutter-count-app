Sony A7R IV — Shutter Rating & High-Resolution Context

The Sony A7R IV (ILCE-7RM4), released in 2019, raised the full-frame resolution bar to 61 megapixels — a threshold that allowed medium-format-quality output from a 35mm sensor for the first time. It was Sony's flagship resolution body until the A7R V launched in 2022. The mechanical shutter is rated at 500,000 actuations, Sony's highest tier, appropriate for a body intended for studio, landscape, and commercial photography where every shot counts.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Sony A7R IV

  1. Take a still photo with your Sony A7R IV using the mechanical shutter and locate the .ARW file.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser.
  3. Drag the ARW file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The mechanical shutter count appears instantly, decrypted from the ARW MakerNote in your browser.

Use a file shot in mechanical shutter mode. The A7R IV's silent / electronic shutter mode does not increment the mechanical counter. As a high-resolution body, many owners use electronic shutter to avoid any vibration from the mechanical curtain at medium shutter speeds.

How Does ShutterCount Read the Sony A7R IV Shutter Count?

The Sony A7R IV stores its mechanical shutter count encrypted in ARW MakerNote tag 0x9050. ShutterCount applies Sony's cubic residue decryption algorithm (modulo 249) and reads the count from byte offset 0x003a within the decrypted block. All decryption and parsing is performed locally in your browser — no file data is ever transmitted.

Sony A7R IV Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Sony A7R IV?

Shoot a mechanical ARW frame and drop it into shuttercount.app. The count is decrypted from tag 0x9050 at offset 0x003a in your browser, instantly and privately.

What is the rated shutter life of the Sony A7R IV?

Sony rates the A7R IV at 500,000 actuations — the same as the A7R V, A7S III, A9 III, and A1.

Is the Sony A7R IV or A7R V worth the price difference?

The A7R V adds significantly better AI subject tracking AF (the difference between reliable and unreliable tracking of moving subjects), a dramatically improved 9.44M-dot EVF, 8-stop IBIS (vs 5.5-stop), and 4K/60p video. For studio and landscape work where the subject is stationary, the A7R IV at a lower used price is excellent value. For wildlife or editorial where tracking matters, the A7R V's AF is transformative.

Does using pixel shift on the A7R IV affect the shutter count?

Yes. Pixel Shift Multi Shooting uses the mechanical shutter to take 4 or 16 consecutive frames per capture. Each frame increments the mechanical counter individually. A photographer who heavily uses pixel shift may have a higher shutter count relative to the number of final composite images.

Can the Sony A7R IV shutter count be reset?

Only Sony authorised service centres can reset the hardware counter. Always verify from an original ARW file, not a screenshot or JPEG.
