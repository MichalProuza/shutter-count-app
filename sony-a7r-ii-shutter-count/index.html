Sony A7R II at a Glance

The Sony A7R II, released in August 2015, was a groundbreaking full-frame mirrorless camera. It introduced the world's first back-illuminated (BSI) full-frame sensor at 42.4 megapixels, combined with 5-axis in-body image stabilization (IBIS) and 399-point phase-detection AF. It quickly became a favorite among landscape, portrait, and fine-art photographers.

The A7R II shutter is rated at 500,000 actuations — the highest tier in Sony's lineup, matching the A9 and A7R III. Understanding the shutter count is critical when buying a used A7R II, as many bodies on the market in 2026 are over 10 years old.

How to Check Sony A7R II Shutter Count

  1. Set your A7R II to single-shot mechanical shutter mode (not silent/electronic).
  2. Take a fresh photo of any subject — indoors is fine.
  3. Transfer the ARW file to your computer via USB or SD card reader.
  4. Go to shuttercount.app and upload the ARW file.
  5. The shutter count appears within seconds — no account or installation needed.
Technical note: Sony embeds shutter actuations in an encrypted MakerNote block (EXIF tag 0x9050). For the A7R II, the count is stored at offset 0x003a using a cubic residue cipher modulo 249. Shuttercount.app decrypts and reads this automatically.

Does Electronic Shutter Affect the Count?

No. The Sony A7R II supports silent electronic shutter shooting, but these exposures do not increment the mechanical shutter counter in the ARW MakerNote. Only physical mechanical shutter actuations are counted.

Buyer warning: If a seller has used the A7R II extensively in silent mode (e.g., for studio or wedding work), the mechanical count will appear lower than the total camera use. Always ask about usage patterns alongside the shutter count figure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the shutter count on a Sony A7R II?

Take a mechanical shutter photo, upload the ARW file to shuttercount.app, and the tool reads the count from the encrypted MakerNote at offset 0x003a. The result is instant and requires no software installation.

What is the Sony A7R II shutter life rating?

Sony rates the A7R II shutter at 500,000 actuations. This is the highest tier rating Sony offers, reflecting the camera's professional-grade build quality.

Does the Sony A7R II shutter count include electronic shutter shots?

No. Only mechanical shutter actuations are counted. Silent electronic shutter shots do not increment the counter in the ARW MakerNote.

Is 50,000 shutter count high for a used Sony A7R II?

No — 50,000 is just 10% of the 500,000 rating. This indicates very light use and is an excellent buy. Counts above 350,000 warrant more scrutiny and a lower asking price.

What is the difference between Sony A7R II and A7R III for shutter count checking?

Both models use the same MakerNote offset 0x003a in the encrypted tag 0x9050 block. Shuttercount.app handles both automatically. Both share the 500,000 shutter rating — the A7R III improves battery and AF but uses the same shutter mechanism encoding.
