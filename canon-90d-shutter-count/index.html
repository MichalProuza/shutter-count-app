It features a 32.5 MP APS-C sensor (the highest-resolution APS-C Canon has ever shipped), uncropped 4K video, 10 fps burst shooting, 45-point all cross-type AF, Dual Pixel AF with eye detection, a fully articulating touchscreen and weather sealing. How to Check Shutter Count on the Canon 90D

The Canon 90D does not display the shutter count in its on-screen menus. The count is written into the MakerNote of every CR3 RAW file.

  1. Take any photo with your Canon 90D and locate the .CR3 file on the SD card.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  3. Drag the CR3 file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The shutter count and remaining life percentage appear instantly. No upload, no account, no plugins required.

How Does ShutterCount Read the 90D Shutter Count?

The Canon 90D writes images in Canon's CR3 format — an ISOBMFF container (the same base format used by MP4 and HEIF). ShutterCount parses the box hierarchy to locate the embedded EXIF block and then reads the Canon MakerNote metadata.

All processing happens in your browser — the file never leaves your device.

Can I use a JPEG from my 90D?

Canon does not reliably embed the shutter counter in JPEG files. Use a CR3 RAW file for accurate results. If you shoot RAW+JPEG, use the CR3 side of the pair.

Does electronic shutter / live-view affect the count?

The 90D's mechanical shutter only fires when taking still photos in the normal shooting mode. Video recording and live-view use do not increment the mechanical shutter count.

Canon 90D Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Canon 90D?

Shoot a CR3 RAW file with your 90D, then drop it into shuttercount.app. The metadata is read entirely in your browser — no upload needed.

What is the rated shutter life of the Canon 90D?

Canon rates the EOS 90D at 120,000 actuations. This is a median estimate; individual shutters may last significantly longer or fail earlier.

Can the shutter count be reset or faked?

Only a Canon authorised service centre can reset the hardware counter after a physical shutter replacement. EXIF-editing tools overwrite metadata but cannot alter the in-camera counter. Always verify from an original CR3 RAW file.

Should I buy the 90D or the mirrorless R7?

Both share a 32.5 MP APS-C sensor and target enthusiast photographers. The R7 offers in-body stabilization, an electronic viewfinder, a 200,000-actuation shutter and access to RF lenses. The 90D gives you an optical viewfinder, longer battery life and direct compatibility with EF/EF-S glass. Choose based on your lens collection and viewfinder preference.

Is the 90D still being produced?

As of 2026, the 90D is still listed in many Canon catalogues but is increasingly being positioned as a niche DSLR alongside Canon's dominant mirrorless lineup. New-old-stock and used bodies remain easy to find.
