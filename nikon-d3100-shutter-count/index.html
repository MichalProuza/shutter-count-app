Nikon D3100 — Shutter Rating & Context

The Nikon D3100 (2010) was Nikon’s first entry-level DSLR to feature Full HD 1080p video recording. Its 14.2 MP CMOS sensor replaced the older CCD design used in the D3000, delivering improved high-ISO performance and live-view capability. The mechanical shutter is rated at 100,000 actuations.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Nikon D3100

  1. Take a still photo with your Nikon D3100 and locate the .NEF file.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser.
  3. Drag the NEF file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The mechanical shutter count appears instantly, read from MakerNote tag 0x00A7 in your browser.

The D3100 does not have a silent or electronic shutter mode — every frame increments the mechanical shutter counter.

How Does ShutterCount Read the Nikon D3100 Shutter Count?

The Nikon D3100 stores the mechanical shutter count in NEF MakerNote EXIF tag 0x00A7 (decimal 167). This is the standard Nikon shutter count field, used identically across virtually all Nikon DSLRs and Z-series mirrorless cameras. ShutterCount reads this tag directly in your browser — no encryption or decryption needed.

100% local: All processing happens in your browser. The NEF file never leaves your device.

Can I use a JPEG instead of a NEF file?

JPEGs from the D3100 do not contain the MakerNote data needed to read the shutter count. Always use the original .NEF raw file for an accurate reading.

Nikon D3100 Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Nikon D3100?

Shoot a NEF frame and drop it into shuttercount.app. The count is read from MakerNote tag 0x00A7 in your browser, instantly and privately.

What is the rated shutter life of the Nikon D3100?

Nikon rates the D3100 at 100,000 actuations. Many units exceed this figure, but the failure risk increases after the rated limit.

Is the Nikon D3100 still worth buying used?

For budget-conscious beginners, the D3100 offers excellent value. It supports the full Nikon F-mount AF-S lens library, produces quality 14.2 MP images, and can be found at very low prices. However, newer models like the D3300 or D3500 offer significant improvements in resolution and image quality.

Can the Nikon D3100 shutter count be reset?

Only Nikon authorised service centres can reset the hardware counter. Always verify from an original NEF file, not a screenshot or JPEG.
