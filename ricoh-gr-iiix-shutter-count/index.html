Ricoh GR IIIx — Shutter Ratings

The Ricoh GR IIIx (2021) is the telephoto companion to the GR III, sharing the same 24.2 MP APS-C sensor and compact body but fitted with a 26.1mm f/2.8 lens (40mm full-frame equivalent). The longer focal length makes it a favourite for street portraits, documentary photography, and tighter environmental shots. Like the GR III, it shoots natively in DNG RAW and stores shutter count in EXIF metadata.

Inspect the lens element for dust ingestion — the retractable lens on unweather-sealed compact cameras is a common ingress point.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Ricoh GR IIIx

The Ricoh GR IIIx shoots RAW files in Adobe DNG format with Ricoh MakerNote EXIF metadata containing the shutter count. ShutterCount reads this directly in your browser:

  1. Set your GR IIIx to shoot in RAW or RAW+JPEG mode and take any photo.
  2. Locate the resulting .DNG file on your SD card or copy it to your computer.
  3. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  4. Drag the DNG file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  5. The shutter count appears instantly. No upload, no account, no plugins required.

You can also use ExifTool: exiftool -ShutterCount image.dng

Tip: When inspecting a used GR IIIx, verify the shutter count from a DNG taken during the in-person inspection. Also check the lens element for dust and verify AF operation — snappy phase-detect AF is a key strength of this camera.

Ricoh GR IIIx Shutter Count — FAQ

How is the GR IIIx different from the GR III?

The only hardware difference is the lens: the GR IIIx uses a 26.1mm f/2.8 (40mm equivalent), making it better suited for portraits and tighter street shots. The GR III has an 18.3mm f/2.8 (28mm equivalent) for wider environmental shots. Both share the same sensor, shutter, body, and DNG format.

What is the rated shutter life of the GR IIIx?

Ricoh does not publish an official rating. The estimated lifespan is approximately 200,000 actuations, consistent with the GR III.

Does the electronic shutter affect the count?

The GR IIIx supports an electronic shutter mode. Electronic shutter exposures do not actuate the mechanical curtain and generally do not increment the mechanical shutter count.
