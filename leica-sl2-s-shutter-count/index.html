Leica SL2-S — Shutter Rating

The Leica SL2-S (2020) is the video-optimised variant of the SL2 series. Where the SL2 prioritises 47.3 MP resolution, the SL2-S trades pixel count for speed and sensitivity: its 24.6 MP BSI CMOS sensor delivers usable images at ISO 50,000, up to 25 fps burst with electronic shutter, and 4K video up to 60 fps (crop). The body is identical to the SL2 — machined aluminium, IP54 dust and splash protection, and 5-axis IBIS rated at 5.5 stops.

Leica does not publish an official mechanical shutter rating for the SL2-S. Based on its professional construction and comparison with the SL2, the shutter life is estimated at approximately 150,000–200,000 actuations.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Leica SL2-S

  1. Via shuttercount.app (DNG): Shoot a photo in DNG format (or DNG+JPEG). Go to shuttercount.app, drag and drop the DNG file onto the tool, and the shutter count appears immediately. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
  2. Via ExifTool: Run exiftool -ShutterCount yourfile.dng in a terminal. The SL2-S reliably embeds the actuation count in DNG MakerNote metadata.
  3. Via camera menu: On the SL2-S, navigate to MENU → Camera Settings → Shutter Count to view the count directly. This is the most convenient method and does not require a RAW file.
  4. When buying used, verify the shutter count via the camera menu or request a DNG file for tool-based confirmation. Also test 4K video recording and IBIS function.

Leica SL2-S — FAQ

What is the difference between the Leica SL2 and SL2-S?

The core difference is the sensor: SL2 has 47.3 MP for maximum detail, while SL2-S has 24.6 MP for superior low-light performance and speed. The SL2-S reaches ISO 100,000 natively (SL2: 50,000), shoots up to 25 fps electronically (SL2: 20 fps), and offers enhanced 4K video with higher frame rates. Both cameras share the same weather-sealed body and L-mount compatibility.

Can I use Leica M lenses on the SL2-S?

Yes, with the Leica M-Adapter L, all Leica M-mount lenses are compatible with the SL2-S. Manual focus with focus peaking and magnification assist is fully supported. IBIS operates with all adapted lenses. Note that shutter count accumulates normally when using adapted M lenses.

Is the Leica SL2-S a good used camera in 2026?

Yes, for photographers and videographers who value the Leica ecosystem, exceptional build quality, and full-frame L-mount performance. The 24.6 MP sensor and 4K video capabilities remain competitive. Used prices are significantly lower than new, making it an attractive option — verify the shutter count and IBIS function before purchase.

Does electronic shutter count on the Leica SL2-S?

The shuttercount.app tool and ExifTool read the mechanical shutter count embedded in DNG files. Electronic-only actuations (silent mode) are generally not included in the mechanical count. If you frequently use the electronic shutter, the mechanical count understates total use, but the shutter mechanism itself is unaffected by electronic actuations.
