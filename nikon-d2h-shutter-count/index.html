Nikon D2H — Shutter Rating & Overview

The Nikon D2H (2003) was Nikon's first integrated-grip professional DSLR built specifically for sports and photojournalism. Featuring an unusual 4.1 MP LBCAST (JFET) sensor — a Nikon-developed alternative to CCD/CMOS — it could shoot up to 8 fps and had the rugged magnesium body that would define the entire D2 / D3 / D4 / D5 / D6 lineage. The shutter is rated at approximately 200,000 actuations.

LBCAST sensor: The D2H is one of the very few cameras to use Nikon's proprietary LBCAST sensor technology. Image character is unique, but ISO performance is very limited by modern standards.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Nikon D2H

  1. Take a still photo with your Nikon D2H and locate the .NEF file.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser.
  3. Drag the NEF file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The mechanical shutter count appears instantly, read from MakerNote tag 0x00A7 in your browser.

How Does ShutterCount Read the Nikon D2H Shutter Count?

The Nikon D2H stores the mechanical shutter count in NEF MakerNote EXIF tag 0x00A7 (decimal 167). This is the standard Nikon shutter count field, used identically across all Nikon DSLRs and Z-series mirrorless cameras. ShutterCount reads this tag directly in your browser — no upload, no decryption required.

100% local: All processing happens in your browser. The NEF file never leaves your device.

Nikon D2H Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Nikon D2H?

Shoot a NEF frame and drop it into shuttercount.app. The count is read from MakerNote tag 0x00A7 in your browser, instantly and privately.

What is the rated shutter life of the Nikon D2H?

Nikon rates the D2H at approximately 200,000 actuations.

What is the difference between the D2H and D2Hs?

The D2Hs (2005) added improved JPEG processing, better white balance, simultaneous RAW + JPEG capture, and a larger buffer. Both use the same 4.1 MP LBCAST sensor and shutter mechanism.

Can the Nikon D2H shutter count be reset?

Only Nikon authorised service centres can reset the hardware counter. Always verify from an original NEF file, not a screenshot or JPEG.
