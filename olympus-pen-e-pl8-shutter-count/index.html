How to Check Shutter Count on the Olympus PEN E-PL8

The Olympus PEN E-PL8 does not display the shutter count anywhere in its menu system. The counter is embedded in the MakerNote of every ORF RAW file the camera produces.

  1. Set your E-PL8 to RAW or RAW + JPEG shooting mode and take at least one photo.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
  3. Drag the resulting .ORF file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The shutter count is shown immediately. No upload, no account, no plugins needed.

All parsing runs entirely on your device via JavaScript. Your file never leaves your computer, preserving your privacy even when checking a camera you are considering buying from a seller.

How Does ShutterCount Read the E-PL8 Shutter Count?

The Olympus PEN E-PL8 writes images in Olympus's ORF format, a TIFF-based container. ShutterCount locates the embedded EXIF block and reads the Olympus MakerNote IFD to extract the shutter actuation counter.

All parsing happens in your browser via JavaScript. Your file is never uploaded; everything stays on your device.

Can I use a JPEG from my E-PL8?

Olympus does not reliably embed the shutter counter in JPEG EXIF on the E-PL8. Use an ORF RAW file for accurate results. Switch the Image Quality setting to RAW or RAW+JPEG and take one new photo before checking.

Olympus PEN E-PL8 vs E-PL9 — what changed?

The E-PL9 (2018) succeeded the E-PL8 with a new 4K video mode, built-in pop-up flash (previously absent on the PL8), and Bluetooth connectivity. Both use the same 16 MP Live MOS sensor and ORF format, and shutter count reads identically from both via shuttercount.app.

Olympus PEN E-PL8 Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Olympus PEN E-PL8?

Shoot an ORF RAW file, then drop it into shuttercount.app. The shutter count is read from the file's MakerNote entirely in your browser — no upload needed.

Does the Olympus PEN E-PL8 have a rated shutter life?

Olympus rated the E-PL8's shutter at approximately 100,000 actuations. This is a statistical median; many units last significantly longer. The PEN E-PL series was designed for lighter shooting patterns than the OM-D professional line.

Are Olympus PEN E-PL8 lenses compatible with OM System cameras?

Yes — all Micro Four Thirds lenses are fully compatible across Olympus PEN, Olympus OM-D, and OM System bodies. The E-PL8 can use every MFT lens ever made, giving it access to an extensive optics ecosystem.

Can the shutter count be reset or faked?

Only an Olympus authorised service centre can reset the hardware counter after a physical shutter replacement. EXIF-editing tools can alter metadata but cannot change the in-camera counter. Always verify from an original, untouched ORF RAW file.
