OM System OM-1 — Shutter Rating

The OM System OM-1 (2022) is the flagship Micro Four Thirds mirrorless camera, succeeding the Olympus E-M1 Mark III. It features a 20.4 MP stacked BSI CMOS sensor with on-chip phase-detection AF, 120 fps blackout-free shooting, computational photography modes (Live ND, handheld high-res), and IP53 weather sealing. The mechanical shutter is rated at an impressive 400,000 actuations — matching many full-frame professional bodies.

How to Check Shutter Count on the OM System OM-1

  1. Via camera menu (recommended): On your OM-1, go to Menu → Wrench icon → Camera Info. The shutter count is displayed directly.
  2. Via ORF file (experimental): Take a photo and drop the .ORF file into shuttercount.app. The tool will attempt to extract shutter count from the EXIF metadata, but results are not guaranteed for OM System bodies.
  3. When buying used, ask the seller to photograph the camera menu screen showing the shutter count.

OM System OM-1 Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my OM System OM-1?

The most reliable method is the camera's built-in menu: Menu → Wrench → Camera Info → Shutter Count. You can also try dropping an ORF file into shuttercount.app, though in-file support is not yet confirmed for OM System bodies.

What is the rated shutter life of the OM System OM-1?

The OM-1 is rated at 400,000 actuations — exceptionally high for a Micro Four Thirds body and on par with full-frame flagships like the Nikon Z8.

Does the electronic shutter affect the count?

No. The OM-1's electronic shutter mode (up to 120 fps) does not use the mechanical shutter and does not increment the actuation counter. Many wildlife and sports photographers use electronic shutter exclusively, resulting in very low mechanical shutter wear.

Is the OM System OM-1 the same as the Olympus OM-1?

No. The OM System OM-1 (2022) is a modern Micro Four Thirds digital mirrorless camera. The original Olympus OM-1 was a 35mm film SLR from 1972. OM System is the brand that succeeded Olympus in the camera business.
