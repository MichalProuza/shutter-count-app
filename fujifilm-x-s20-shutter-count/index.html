Fujifilm X-S20 — Shutter Rating

The Fujifilm X-S20 is Fujifilm's 2023 mid-range hybrid APS-C mirrorless camera, featuring a 26.1 MP X-Trans CMOS 5 LM sensor with 7-stop IBIS, 6.2K video recording, and a fully articulating touchscreen. It targets travel and content-creation photographers who want Fujifilm's film simulations and compact ergonomics in a versatile photo/video body. Its 150,000-actuation shutter rating places it between the entry-level X100VI (150,000) and the higher-tier X-T5 (300,000).

Video use context: The X-S20 is frequently used as a video camera — it supports 6.2K open-gate recording and is popular with content creators. Video recording does not increment the mechanical shutter counter. An X-S20 used primarily for video may have a very low mechanical count regardless of actual usage hours. Factor this in when evaluating a used body.

How to Check Shutter Count on the Fujifilm X-S20

  1. Take a still photo with your Fujifilm X-S20 using the mechanical shutter and locate the .RAF file.
  2. Open shuttercount.app in any modern browser.
  3. Drag the RAF file onto the drop zone, or click to open a file picker.
  4. The shutter count and remaining life percentage appear instantly. No upload, no account required.
Counter reset warning: RAF MakerNote tag 0x1438 may reset to zero after formatting the memory card. This is a known Fujifilm limitation. The physical shutter hardware counter is unaffected. Always verify from a recent RAF file when evaluating a used X-S20.

Fujifilm X-S20 Shutter Count — FAQ

How do I check the shutter count of my Fujifilm X-S20?

Shoot a mechanical RAF frame, then drop it into shuttercount.app. The count is read from tag 0x1438 in your browser.

What is the rated shutter life of the Fujifilm X-S20?

Fujifilm rates the X-S20 at 150,000 actuations — unchanged from the X-S10.

Why does the X-S20 have a lower rating than the X-T5?

The X-T5 uses a higher-tier shutter mechanism suited to its traditional photo-first design. The X-S20's shutter is sized for a hybrid content-creation camera where video shoots (which don't increment the count) form a significant portion of use.

Can the X-S20 shutter count be reset?

The RAF image counter resets after card formatting. The hardware shutter counter can only be reset by a Fujifilm authorised service centre after shutter replacement.
